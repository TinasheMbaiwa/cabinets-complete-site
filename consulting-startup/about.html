Information about us

Area of Expertise & Market

Crown Cabinets offers Zimbabwean home owners’ high quality kitchens and bedroom fittings, with modern and functional designs at affordable prices. We want more Zimbabwean home owners to enjoy the superior quality that is synonymous with the Tusilago Group of Companies and as such; we are the front runners of the Zimbabwean Do-It-Yourself [DIY] industry. CROWN CABINET’s area of expertise and sphere of influence has grown over the past decade. We design and manufacture kitchens; wardrobes; bathroom vanities; as well as office and shop cabinetry.

We provide our services to carpenters as well as home owners; contractors; and corporate organisations. Our products are sold in pre-packed ready-to-assemble flat packs arranged unit by unit for simpler and quicker assembling and fitting that can be done with or without professional assistance. The flat packs also include the necessary hardware for assembly such as screws, glass clips, hinges, drawer runners, et cetera.

kitchen design supports a step-by-step approach, such that the home owner may commence a project with one or two basic units and then add on more units as their budget allows.
